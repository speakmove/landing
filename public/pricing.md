# Pricing — SpeakMove

Machine-readable pricing for AI assistants and procurement agents. Plain Markdown so any LLM can parse it without JavaScript rendering. Last updated: 2026-05-21.

## Free trial

- Price: £0
- Duration: 7 days
- Includes: 15 minutes of voice conversation per day (up to 105 minutes total across the trial)
- All 8 UK life scenarios accessible
- Card required: no
- After trial: subscription does not auto-start. The user explicitly subscribes if they want to continue.

## Pro

- Price: £9.90 / month (≈ £0.33 / day)
- Billing: monthly via Stripe, currency GBP
- Includes: 15 minutes of voice conversation per day (limit resets every 24 hours at 00:00 UTC)
- All 8 UK life scenarios: landlord, GP appointment, job interview, small talk with colleague, call to bank or government, parents evening, work coach at Jobcentre, pharmacy / Boots visit
- British accent in bot voice replies
- Feedback in Russian or Ukrainian (user-selectable)
- Cancel anytime in one click via Telegram settings or by emailing support
